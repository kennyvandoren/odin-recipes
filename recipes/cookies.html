Macadamia Nut Chocolate Chip Cookies

Drop cookies with macadamian nuts and chocolate chips.

This recipe takes a total of 40 minutes to prepare and has 36 servings.

There are 109 calories, 7.3 g of fat, and 71.4 mg of sodium per serving.

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/8 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup macadamian nutes, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degress F. Lightly grease 2 large cookie sheets with vegetable shortening.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars together in a large bowl.
  3. Beat the egg and vanilla extract until well blended.
  4. Sift togther the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually blend into the batter.
  5. Stur in the chopped macadamian nuts and chocolate chips. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto the cookie sheets, about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Remove form the oven and transfer cookies to cooling racks.
