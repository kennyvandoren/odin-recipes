Marshmellow Brownies

Butterscotch choolate marshmellow brownies.

This recipe has 12 servings and will yield 2 dozen.

There are 448 calories, 21.4 g of fat, and 293 mg of sodium per serving.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup butterscotch chips
  • 1/2 cup butter
    • <1i>1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups miniature marshmellows
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degress F. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. Melt butterscotch morsels and butter in a large bowl in microwave.
  3. Stir in the mixture wella nd let it cool to lukewarm.
  4. While the liquid mixure is cooling, mix flour, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, vanilla, and eggs into the butterscotch mixture; mix well.
  5. Fold in marshmellows and chocolate morsels.
  6. Spread batter into a lightly greased 9x13 inch pan.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes.
