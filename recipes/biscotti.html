Cinnamon Hazelnut Biscotti

This is a great treat with coffee and they also smell very good.

This recipe takes a total of 1 hour and 35 minutes to prepare and has 30 servings.

There are 138 calories, 7.8 g of fat, and 88.5 mg of sodium per serving.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground Cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup hazelnuts
